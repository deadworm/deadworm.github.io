Me

This guy got his physics bachelor degree at Nankai University in 2019. He is proud of having alumnus as Shiing-shen Chern (known for Chern number), Wu Ta-You (whose illustrious students include Chen Ning Yang and Tsung-Dao Lee), and hope he can also make some contribution deserved to mention. Obviously, he still has a long way to go.

He was luckily supervised by Dr. Jiangping Hu when he was undergraduate where he learned the important taste and recipe of researching. He again luckily found a PhD position in the University of Hong Kong advised by Dr. Zi Yang Meng during the Covid period after gapping a year as RA in IOP.

His main interest focuses on many-body physics. He would like to solve a problem both numerically and analytically and he is also interested in experimentally related problems. (Read this CV to learn more about him)
